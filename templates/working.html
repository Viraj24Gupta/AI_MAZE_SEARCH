WORKING OF OUR PROJECT

In this project we have implemented a-star and ida-star for finding the shortest and the most optimal
path from one point to another point in the maze.
The maze is made of 0's and 1's where the 1's act as the path blocker and the traversal path is defined by the 0's.
We have made use of two movements i.e. DOWN(D) and RIGHT(R).
